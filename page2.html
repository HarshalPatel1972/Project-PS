~ Care to dance under the stars with me as my adored Ball Partner? ~

* A ball party at the National Defense Academy (NDA) is a grand
and formal event, steeped in tradition and camaraderie. On this
prestigious day, cadets invite their partners to join them for a
night of celebration marked by a formal dinner, dancing, and
entertainment. It's an elegant affair that showcases the spirit of
the military community, providing an opportunity to create
lasting memories in a refined setting.
